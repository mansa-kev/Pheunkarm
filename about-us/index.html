About Pheunkarm

Pheunkarm Institute of Trading & Portfolio Management is dedicated to helping aspiring traders build the knowledge, charting skills, and confidence needed to navigate the global financial markets. As a premier provider of financial education, we deliver student-centric programs designed by traders, for traders.

Our teaching approach focuses heavily on practical execution. We believe that real experience beats theoretical testing, which is why we guide students through live charting sessions and simulated execution drills to cultivate professional risk habits and solid trading psychology.

By leveraging the diverse backgrounds of our global student base, we foster a rich network of retail investors who discuss, evaluate, and backtest trading plans daily.

"Our mission is to democratize financial markets education. We deliver focused, practical training that equips students with real-world skills to manage capital with confidence."

Pheunkarm Institute of Trading & Portfolio Management Mission Statement

Our Virtual Trading Environment

While we operate entirely online, we preserve the collaborative energy of a physical trading floor through our modern platform integrations.

Virtual Trading Floor

Every student receives access to professional-grade charting setups, live data feeds, and advanced simulator accounts to practice analysis tools in real-time.

Interactive Live Streams

Participate in daily market reviews where our senior coaches parse price movements, evaluate current events, and map potential key support and resistance zones.

Student Network Hub

Share, debate, and backtest trading plans inside our secure chat channels, keeping you connected to peers and mentors even after course hours.
